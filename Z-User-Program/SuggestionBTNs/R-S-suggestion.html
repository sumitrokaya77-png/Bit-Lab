Why does it happen?

Heavy menstrual bleeding may occur due to hormonal changes, certain medical conditions, or natural variation. Choosing suitable menstrual products can improve comfort and reduce leaks.

Suggested Sanitation & Self-Care
  • 🌸 Use High-Absorbency Pads for greater protection.
  • 🌸 Use Overnight Pads while sleeping.
  • 🌸 Use a Large-Capacity Menstrual Cup if comfortable and appropriate.
  • 🌸 Wear Period Underwear for additional leak protection.
  • 🌸 Change menstrual products regularly according to product instructions.
  • 🌸 Wear breathable cotton underwear to reduce moisture buildup.

The uterus contracts to remove its lining during menstruation. Stronger contractions can cause cramping.

Why does it happen?

Moisture, friction, prolonged use of menstrual products, or sensitivity to certain materials may irritate the skin.

Suggested Sanitation & Self-Care
  • 🌸 Change pads regularly.
  • 🌸 Wear breathable cotton underwear.
  • 🌸 Avoid scented menstrual products.
  • 🌸 Wash the external genital area with warm water and dry gently.
  • 🌸 Avoid tight-fitting clothing.
Why does it happen?

Menstrual blood itself has little odor. Odor usually develops when blood remains exposed to air for extended periods. A strong or foul odor may indicate an infection.

Suggested Sanitation & Self-Care
  • 🌸 Change menstrual products regularly.
  • 🌸 Bathe or shower daily.
  • 🌸 Change underwear daily.
  • 🌸 Keep the external genital area clean and dry.
  • 🌸 Avoid vaginal douching unless advised by a healthcare professional.
Why does it happen?

Limited access to menstrual supplies or restrooms can make period management more difficult.

Suggested Sanitation & Self-Care
  • 🌸 Carry a small Period Kit containing: Extra pads/tampons, panty liners, spare underwear, disposal bags, and hand sanitizer.
  • 🌸 Check and change menstrual products during breaks.
  • 🌸 Wear comfortable, breathable clothing.
Why does it happen?

Some individuals have skin that is more sensitive to adhesives, fragrances, or materials used in menstrual products.

Suggested Sanitation & Self-Care
  • 🌸 Choose fragrance-free menstrual products.
  • 🌸 Wear soft cotton underwear.
  • 🌸 Avoid tight clothing that causes friction.
  • 🌸 Wash the external genital area gently with warm water.
  • 🌸 Change menstrual products regularly to reduce moisture and irritation.
